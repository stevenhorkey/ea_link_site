FAQ

What genres and styles do you work with?
All kinds of rock, R&B/soul, blues, folk, pop and many more! I am very versatile when it comes to vocals, range, and dynamics, but if you are looking for a particularly high range voice outside of falsetto, then you may want to look elsewhere. Feel free to contact me with more information about your song to make sure that we are the right fit.
How do royalties and credit work for the songs you work on?
All royalties belong to you. If it's a cover song, you are responsible for handling any outside royalties or legal matters on your own. As for credit, check with me on a song-by-song basis.
What if I need my song ready by a specific date?
Usually, this is perfectly fine! I can work with you to make sure that your song is ready whenever you need it. In your order form, let me know what the timeline is, and we can arrange the delivery accordingly. Keep in mind that I am on Mountain Standard Time (Phoenix, AZ USA).
What if my song has explicit lyrics or content?
Usually not a problem at all, but I find that this is best approached on a song-by-song basis. Contact me with your ideas and the lyrics or content in question, and we can figure out the best way to proceed.
What if I want something changed on the tracks you deliver?
Simply put: I will work on your song until you are 110% satisfied. I have an unlimited revisions policy because I want to ensure you that you will walk away happy with the final product.
What equipment do you record with?
For guitar, I utilize a custom-built "vintage modern" Stratocaster, a high-end Yamaha steel-string acoustic, and a Gretch lap steel. I run them through vintage-style and contemporary Marshall amps along with a combination of analog and digital effects.

For vocals, I use professional Shure Microphones along with Mackie preamps and run everything through Logic Pro X with both stock and premium plugins.
I can't find something that I'm looking for on your site.
No worries - if you have any questions AT ALL, don't hesitate to send me a message and I will get back to you as soon as possible. I am very flexible with orders and want to make sure your specific needs are met.

Why Hire Me?

I get it, I've hired session musicians before, and it can be a hit-or-miss process. Some are excellent players and communicators, and others not so much.

The sound of some performers fit with a project, and others don't - that's just the nature of art. But even before that, what's just as important is whether or not the performer gives their 100% to the music and is doing so from the right place.

Simply put, there are musicians, and then there are artists.

With absolutely all of the tracks I work on, I aspire to be the latter.

If you are like me, you want your music to exceed the expectations of yourself and your audience.

You want all the effort and late nights to amount to something that expresses who you are and what you are about.

More than anything, you want to share that with the people in your life that matter most, and you want other people to become fans because they feel the depth of what you feel.

Depending on the sound you are going for, I may or may not be the right fit; only you can decide that.

But if you like what you hear, and you want someone who can immerse themselves in your music instead of just 'playing on it', then you've come to the right place.

My main goal is to offer whatever I can to take your song to the next level; to make sure that the sound, attitude, and emotions you envision come through loud and clear.

Until that's happened, my job isn't done, and that's why I only offer an unlimited revisions policy: to make sure that you are completely satisfied with what you hear.

Best wishes and never stop playing,

Steven Horkey
